Destruction Junket

ABOUT


Destruction Junkett is a community organized art collective and critique group. We regularly host workshops, critiques, meditation sessions and discussion groups. Anyone in the community can offer to host an event or critique, please review our Calendar page to review the upcoming events we have, or to offer to host one.

We have a Session Script we read before each meeting to establish some of rules in a space, and to establish some of the principles of our group. On a semi-regular basis we review this and add changes if we see fit.

Occaisionally we put on group shows as a collective and engage in service oriented workshops for the community if you would like to get incolved with any aspects of this please email:

destruction junket AT gmail DOT com

If you would like to be added to our email group where post about upcoming events and workshops please fill out this Form and we will add you.
